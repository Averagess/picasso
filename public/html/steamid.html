What is an SteamID and what are they used for?

SteamID is an unique string of characters and numbers used for

identifying an unique player in the Steam network.

SteamID can be of many different types, and other types

can be more common in other communities.

How do i use this?

To use the tool, you need to get the community profile URL for

the account you want to get the SteamID's for. Copy the URL and paste

it into the text bar at the start of the page and press enter.
